Frequently Asked Questions

❔ How do I confirm availability?

Submit your booking. We confirm within 24 hours via email or Messenger. You'll receive an email when the status changes to Confirmed.

❔ What does the AI Menu Recommendation do?

It analyzes our past confirmed bookings and picks the most-chosen dishes for each category — basically the "crowd favorites" combo. Just tap Apply, then customize anything you want.

❔ How do I use the venue map?

Tap the 📍 Map button beside the venue field. You can search for a place name, tap 🎯 for GPS, or just tap anywhere on the map to drop a pin. The exact address fills in automatically.

❔ Can I pay 100% upfront?

Yes! On the payment step, choose "Full Payment" or enter any custom amount above 50%. The QR codes update automatically.

❔ What does the QR code do?

Open GCash, tap "Pay QR", and scan the QR. It auto-fills our account so you just enter the amount and confirm. BPI app works similarly.

❔ Can I change my menu?

Yes, up to 7 days before your event. Contact us via Facebook Messenger.

❔ What about Lechon?

We offer whole roasted Lechon (25–30 kgs) for ₱9,000. Tick the Lechon checkbox in Step 1.

❔ What if guest count changes?

Use Additional Orders to add extra food at ₱80/head, in 15–30 pax blocks. Confirm count 5 days before event.

❔ Are there hidden charges?

Absolutely none. Package price is all-inclusive: food, table setup, waiters, decor, and chafing dishes.

❔ Do you set up at any venue?

Yes! Just ensure venue access 3 hours before event start time.

❔ Will I be asked for feedback?

Yes — after your event, we send a thank-you email with a link to leave a review. If you approve, we may feature your testimonial on this booking page!
