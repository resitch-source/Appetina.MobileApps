📌 Booking & Reservation

A reservation is confirmed only upon receipt of the 50% downpayment. Appetina reserves the right to decline bookings on dates already reserved.

💰 Payment Terms

Downpayment: 50% required upon booking.
Balance: Remaining 50% must be settled one (1) day before the event. No balance collection on event day.

❌ Cancellation Policy

30+ days before event: refundable minus processing fees. Within 30 days: non-refundable. Rescheduling subject to availability.

🔢 Guest Count

Final count confirmed 5 days before event. Additional guests charged at prevailing per-head rate.

Menu selections final 7 days before event. All equipment remains Appetina property.

📍 Venue & Setup

Venue access required 3 hours before event start. Appetina not liable for venue restrictions.

📷 Photography

Appetina may photograph setup and food for marketing unless client requests otherwise in writing.
